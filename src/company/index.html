Solcata invests worldwide in strategically located infrastructure to streamline commodity supply chains and make trading more efficient. If infra­structure is there already we invest to improve it. Where it doesn’t exist we build it ourselves.

And we have a long-term perspective. We act as part­ner to nations, corporations and communities.

As one of the world's largest independent commodity trading houses Solcata is at the forefront of our industry. We use market knowledge, logistics and global resources to move physical commodities where they’re needed when they’re needed. We develop markets, invest in infrastructure, build bridges and cement alliances to reduce the cost of trading and increase service levels.

We have built a world-class international business by stressing service and performance. Solcata trades commodities in volume and across the world. With offices in 36 countries on six continents our network extends to every corner of the globe. We have developed industrial assets and strategic infrastructure to support our trading activities. Each of our offices has access to the knowledge, resources and support of the Trafigura Group.
